frequently asked questions

1) Shall I have more than one registered application?

No, you should not have more than confirmed application registration. 'Confirmed application' means applications that are completed by mobile number verification. Periodically we review and remove duplicate registrations, it may be liable to reject all the applications you registered if you have more than one.

2) Shall I modify the post preference or any information after registration?

Yes, you can modify the application or alter the post preference anytime by logging into our Portal.Please note, it will take upto 24 hours for changes to take effect on Recruiter's search.

3) Shall I have to apply for each recruitment notification separately?

No, you do not need to submit application for each recruitment notification separately. Once you registered, it will be listed in the recruiter's search based on the criteria they set for selection.

4) I have not received any verification SMS while registering, Is my application registered?

Your application will be treated as registered only if Mobile Number verification by SMS is complete.You can try again by clicking the 'Resend SMS' button to get your verification code that will appear after few seconds. If you still have trouble with SMS verification, there may be an issue with your Mobile Operator. Click on the change Mobile Number link and provide another mobile number. If you don't have any other number, choose verify later option. Once you received the code. You can login and activate your application by entering the verification code in Dashboard.

5) How can I ensure that my Application registered successfully?

After successful completion of the registration we send Registration number & one-time password to login.

6) I have not received registration successful message, what to do?

After successful completion of the registration we send Registration number & One-time password to login. If you have not received this message, try password reset option page with the help of your E-mail address or the Registration number that noted at the time of registration.

7) How can I get notified about the selection?

You will receive notification by SMS and E-mail . You must have a verified E-mail address to get notification by E-mail. After receiving the notification, you can contact the concerned school to get the information regarding your interview and selection procedures. Contact details of the school will be sent via SMS.
